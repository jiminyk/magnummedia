Who We Are

Magnum Media is a leading independent media company, providing a growing array of transit and out-of-home advertising opportunities in South Florida.  We partner with agencies and both local and national clients, delivering relevant high-impact campaigns that connect with consumers.  We have an inventory of over 500 vehicles with 24/7 coverage of the most coveted areas in the Miami-Fort Lauderdale DMA.

We are continuously increasing our inventory so that we can offer an unparalleled mix of advertising options in South Florida.

We are a committed team of innovative professionals who act as your strategic partner, making your marketing goals our own. We understand the consumer journey and are dedicated to executing an out-of-home campaign that will leave a MAGNUM IMPRESSION!
